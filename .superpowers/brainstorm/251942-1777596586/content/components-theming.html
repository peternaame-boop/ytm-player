where the theme switcher lives — REVISED
Killed as a site-wide chrome dropdown (designer agent's call — reads gimmicky, dilutes brand, every theme looks half-baked because none gets the real polish pass).

Reframed as the demo of the theming feature itself. The switcher lives ONLY inside the theme.toml playground in the Configure tab / /docs/configure page. Switching a preset re-skins the mock track row (already in the prototype) AND a small "site preview strip" showing what the rest of the site would look like in that theme.

Site itself stays locked to the ytm coral palette. That's your brand. Charm.sh is always Charm-pink. Be Charm.
TUI flourishes worth keeping (per designer agent): kbd chips as styled <kbd> not images, blinking block cursor in hero only (never repeated elsewhere — becomes noise), ASCII section dividers ── 03 ── KEYBINDINGS ──────────, status-bar footer pinned to viewport bottom showing NORMAL ytm-player v1.9.2 ● 17 sections. Skipped: CRT curvature, scanlines, phosphor glow, matrix rain, glitch effects, fake CLI prompts wrapping marketing copy.
